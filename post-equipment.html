Essential Tools for Home Baking (and what you can skip)

Must-Haves

  • Digital kitchen scale — more accurate than volume measurements
  • Rubber spatula — for scraping bowls
  • Metal whisk — for batters and emulsions
  • Non-stick pan or griddle for pancakes
  • Oven thermometer

Nice-to-Haves

  • Stand mixer (or good hand mixer)
  • Silicone mats
  • Bench scraper

What to skip at first

Specialized expensive tools like a fancy crepe pan or multiple piping tips — buy later when you know you’ll use them.
